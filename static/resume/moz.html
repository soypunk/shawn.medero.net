Objectives & Summary

I want to have a positive impact on the lives of people who use the Internet to make amazing products, communicate with friends and family, or change the world for the better.

I bring more than 15 years of experience in taking advantage of all the Internet has to offer. My experiences are drawn from user experience design, software engineering, project management, product management, community engagement and people development.
